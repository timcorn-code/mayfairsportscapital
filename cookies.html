Cookie Policy

Effective date: 24 March 2026

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

2. How we use cookies

Our website uses a limited number of cookies to ensure the site functions correctly, to understand how visitors interact with our pages, and to support our marketing tools. We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioural tracking across other sites.

4. Third-party cookies

We use Google Fonts to serve typefaces on this website. Google may set cookies or use other tracking technologies when loading font files. For details, refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

5. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or regulation. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us via our contact form.
